I am a Ph. D. candidate in computer science at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. I work at the Interactive Robotics and Vision Lab and supervised by Prof. Junaed Sattar. My research mainly focuses on Visual SLAM, State Estimation, and Robotics. My goal is to improve the efficiency and robustness of SLAM systems, especially in challenging scenarios (e.g., underwater). I am also interested in utilizing deep learning to help SLAM systems.
